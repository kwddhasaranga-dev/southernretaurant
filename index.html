The Heart of Southern Hospitality

Located in the heart of Ambalantota, Southern Restaurant has been serving authentic Southern Sri Lankan cuisine since 2009. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing you the true taste of the South.

From our signature Kottu to freshly caught seafood, every dish is prepared with locally sourced ingredients and traditional spices. Whether you dine in or take away, we promise a culinary experience that feels like home.
